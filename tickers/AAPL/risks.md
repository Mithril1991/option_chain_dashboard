## Key Risks

### China Risk
**Severity**: HIGH | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- 20-25% of revenue from China; geopolitical and regulatory exposure
- iPhone restrictions or ban would impact $50B+ annual revenue
- Local competitors (Huawei, Xiaomi) intense on price
- Impact: 15-20% stock decline on severe restrictions

### iPhone Cycle Saturation
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: HIGH
- Smartphone market mature; growth limited to replacement cycles
- Upgrade cycles extending (customers keeping phones 4-5 years)
- Impact: Revenue growth limited to 3-5% without new categories

### Services Execution
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- Services growth target aggressive (10-15%); must sustain
- Customer retention critical; competition (Spotify, Google) intense
- Impact: If growth slows, multiple compression on lower recurring revenue

### Macro Sensitivity
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- Premium products vulnerable to recession; consumers cut discretionary spending
- Higher interest rates reduce attractiveness of large cash hoard
- Impact: Consumer pullback = revenue miss; multiple de-rating

### Valuation Risk
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- Trading at premium to market (20x forward vs. 16x market)
- Any growth disappointment = multiple compression
- Impact: 15-20% downside on valuation reset

## Risk Mitigation

### For Long Positions
- Position size: 3-5% (lower risk than growth tech)
- Use Put Spreads for downside protection
- Monitor China headlines; be ready to reduce on geopolitical escalation
- Rebalance on 20% gains (take profits on momentum)

### For Options Traders
- Iron Condors work better than straddles (lower volatility)
- Sell Call Spreads for income; cap upside but limit downside
- Avoid naked shorts; China headline risk too binary
- Collar strategies (long stock + short calls + long puts) suitable
