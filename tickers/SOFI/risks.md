## Regulatory Risks

### Banking Charter Compliance
**Severity**: HIGH | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- **Description**: As a bank holding company, SOFI faces heightened regulatory scrutiny from Federal Reserve, OCC, and FDIC
- **Impact**: Increased compliance costs, potential operational restrictions, slower product launches
- **Monitor**: Regulatory announcements, stress test results, capital requirements
- **Mitigation**: Strong capital adequacy, proactive compliance programs

### Consumer Protection Regulations
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- **Description**: Changes to consumer lending laws (TILA-RESPA, Equal Credit Opportunity Act) could impact business model
- **Impact**: Higher origination costs, restricted lending practices, potential fines
- **Monitor**: Congressional activity, regulatory rulemaking, enforcement actions against peers
- **Example**: 2023 FDIC enforcement concerns around deposit insurance advertising

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Compliance
**Severity**: HIGH | **Probability**: LOW
- **Description**: Fintech companies face enhanced AML/KYC requirements; violations carry heavy penalties
- **Impact**: Suspension of services, fines exceeding $100M+, reputational damage
- **Monitor**: FinCEN announcements, suspicious activity patterns
- **Precedent**: LendingClub and other fintechs have faced AML-related fines

## Earnings Risks

### Profitability Delays
**Severity**: HIGH | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- **Description**: SOFI has guided toward profitability but execution risks remain; cost structure may not scale as expected
- **Impact**: Guidance misses, margin compression, multiple contraction
- **Monitor**: Quarterly operating expenses, revenue growth rates, member acquisition costs

### Interest Rate Sensitivity
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: HIGH
- **Description**: SOFI's lending business benefits from rising rates but is exposed to refinancing risks in falling rate environment
- **Impact**: Lower loan origination volumes, compressed spreads, deposit funding cost increases
- **Monitor**: Fed rate expectations, Treasury yield curve, mortgage volume data

### Member Acquisition Cost (MAC) Inflation
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- **Description**: Increased competition could drive up customer acquisition spending
- **Impact**: Delayed profitability, lower lifetime value per customer
- **Monitor**: Marketing spend trends, member growth rates, unit economics by cohort

### Credit Deterioration
**Severity**: MEDIUM | **Probability**: MEDIUM
- **Description**: Recession or unemployment spike could increase loan delinquencies and charge-offs
- **Impact**: Higher loan loss reserves, provision expenses, credit quality concerns
- **Monitor**: Economic indicators, unemployment rates, delinquency rates (30+, 60+, 90+ DPD)

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### For Long Positions
1. **Stop Loss**: Set at -20% from entry or key technical support
2. **Position Sizing**: Keep SOFI at 2-5% of portfolio (high risk)
3. **Diversification**: Balance fintech exposure with traditional finance
4. **Hedging**: Consider Put Spreads or Collar strategies before earnings

### For Options Traders
1. **Avoid naked short calls** during regulatory events
2. **Use Put Spreads** to define downside risk
3. **Iron Condors** work best in low-volatility periods post-earnings
4. **Close positions** before major catalyst events if uncertain
